Ingredients:
 - 2 links hot and/or sweet Italian sausage
 - 1/2 red bell pepper, cut into 1/2-inch-wide strips
 - 1/2 red bell pepper, cut into 1/2-inch-wide strips
 - 1 small onion, cut into 1/2-inch wedges
 - 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
 - 3/4 cup Arborio or other medium-grain rice or converted rice
 - 1/2 cup dry white wine
 - 1 cup drained canned tomatoes, chopped coarse
 - 1 1/2 cups water

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 400°F
 2. Squeeze sausage meat from casings into a 10-inch heavy ovenproof skillet
 3. Add bell peppers, onion, and oil
 4. Cook mixture over moderately high heat, breaking up sausage with a fork and
    stirring occasionally, 5 minutes, or until vegetables begin to brown
 5. Add rice and sauté, stirring, 1 minute
 6. Stir in wine, tomatoes, and water and bring to a boil, stirring to loosen
    brown bits
 7. Transfer skillet to oven and bake, uncovered, 25 minutes, or until most
    liquid is absorbed
 8. Season with salt and pepper
